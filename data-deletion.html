Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: April 29, 2026

AuraFlow is operated by Neway Marketing Enterprises. If you would like us to delete account data, workspace data, or Meta-connected records associated with your use of AuraFlow, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to godwillgodslife@gmail.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the workspace name and the data you want removed. If the request relates to Meta, include the connected Facebook Page, lead form, Instagram account, Messenger identity, or WhatsApp number where relevant.
  3. If you are requesting deletion on behalf of a customer record, include enough information for us to locate the record, such as name, email address, phone number, or lead capture date.
  4. We may ask for additional information to verify that the request is authorized before any deletion is processed.

What Happens Next

After verification, we will delete or anonymize applicable data within a reasonable period, unless we must retain some records for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute handling, or operational auditability.

Scope of Deletion

  • Workspace account records may be deleted or deactivated.
  • Lead and messaging records may be deleted or anonymized where applicable.
  • Connected provider credentials are removed when the workspace disconnects the integration.

Contact

For questions about deletion requests, contact godwillgodslife@gmail.com.
