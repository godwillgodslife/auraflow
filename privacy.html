AuraFlow

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

AuraFlow is owned and operated by Neway Marketing Enterprises. AuraFlow helps businesses manage customer communication, lead capture, and follow-up across channels including email, Facebook Lead Ads, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and WhatsApp. This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, why we process it, and how we protect it.

Information We Process

We may process workspace account details, team member records, connected provider metadata, customer contact information, messages, lead form submissions, delivery events, workflow notes, and operational logs needed to run the service.

  • Account and workspace information provided by the business using AuraFlow.
  • Customer contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and company when supplied.
  • Conversation content received through connected messaging and email channels.
  • Facebook Lead Ads field submissions, including form name, campaign context, name, email, and phone when provided by the lead.
  • WhatsApp business messaging data needed to receive, display, route, and respond to customer conversations.

How We Use Data

  • To authenticate users and provide access to the correct workspace.
  • To receive and display customer messages and lead submissions inside AuraFlow.
  • To route conversations, assign owners, manage follow-up, and support customer service workflows.
  • To generate AI-assisted summaries, classifications, recommendations, and draft responses.
  • To monitor reliability, security, abuse prevention, and platform performance.

Facebook Lead Ads and Meta Platform Data

When a business connects Meta services to AuraFlow, we process information returned through Facebook Lead Ads, Messenger, and related Meta webhooks only to deliver the business workflow requested by the workspace owner. Lead data is used to identify the customer, create or update the correct lead record, and support outreach or handoff within that business workspace.

WhatsApp Data

AuraFlow processes WhatsApp message data solely for business communication purposes. This includes receiving inbound messages, showing them to authorized workspace users, supporting replies, preserving conversation history, and coordinating follow-up or escalation where needed.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal data. We may share information only with infrastructure and service providers that help us operate AuraFlow, such as hosting, database, AI, messaging, and deployment vendors, and only to the extent necessary to provide the service securely and reliably.

Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as needed to operate the service, maintain security, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and preserve necessary business communication records.

Data Security

We use access controls, workspace scoping, encrypted secrets storage, and operational safeguards designed to protect customer and workspace data from unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of applicable personal data associated with your use of AuraFlow, subject to identity verification and any legal retention requirements.
